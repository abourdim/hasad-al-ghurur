# حصاد الغرور — The Harvest of Arrogance — La Moisson de l'Orgueil

Interactive trilingual web app based on **"Hasad Al-Ghurur"** (The Harvest of Arrogance) by **Sheikh Mohammed al-Ghazali** (1917-1996).

Published by **Dar Al-Qalam Damascus**. Written after the 1967 defeat (the Naksa).

## Features

- **20 historical lessons** on humility, arrogance, and their consequences
- **6 historical scenes** — nations that fell through arrogance and rose through humility
- **"Who Wants to Be a Scholar?" quiz** — 15 questions with lifelines (50/50, Guidance, Ask the Sheikh)
- **Humility Meter** — self-assessment tool with visual gauge
- **XP & Badges system** — gamification with 6 badges to earn
- **Trilingual** — Arabic, English, French
- **3 themes** — Ember, Night, Desert
- **Narrator** — audiobook mode with karaoke, speed/pitch control, duo reading
- **PWA** — installable, works offline

## Book Themes

- Egypt after the Naksa (1967 defeat)
- Arrogance and distance from God leading to defeat
- Historical parallels (Islamic states fell but rose again)
- Call for a new generation carrying Islam's trust
- Palestine and the Zionist occupation
- Return to authentic Islam
- Cultural invasion weakening Muslims
- Islam in Indonesia (spread through humility, not sword)

## Tech

Pure HTML/CSS/JS — no frameworks, no build tools. Faithful to the existing Al-Ghazali apps design.
